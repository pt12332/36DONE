About 36 Foods

From Vietnam to the world — our story

Founded in 2012, Phuong Hong Dieu Company Limited started as a domestic food manufacturer serving the Vietnamese market. For over a decade we built expertise in dried and frozen food production — sourcing quality ingredients, refining our processes, and earning food safety certifications. In 2025 we made our first international move, exporting to the United States. This is just the beginning — our goal is to bring authentic Vietnamese food products to markets worldwide.

  • Established 2012 — over 13 years of food production experience
  • Quality-sourced ingredients, carefully selected by our experienced team
  • HACCP, ISO 22000 and VSATTP certified production facilities
  • First export to the United States in 2025 — expanding worldwide
